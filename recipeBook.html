Kraft Dinner

  • Time: 15
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Kraft Dinner Box 1
Water 1000mL

Step 1: Add water to pot.

Step 2: Bring Pot to boil.

Step 3: Once water is boiling, add KD noodles.

Step 4: After 12 minutes, strain noodles and add noodles to bowl.

Step 5: Add sauce packet and milk before serving.
